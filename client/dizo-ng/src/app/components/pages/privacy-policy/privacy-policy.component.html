Information We Collect

We gather various types of information concerning you. In the previous 12 months, we may have acquired the following information:

Location: To determine your geographic location, we employ various technologies, such as collecting your IP address or other device-related details, contingent upon your device's location service settings.

Device Information: When you interact with our Platforms or online advertisements, open our emails, or visit us, we learn about your browser, device type, IP address, internet service provider, and other technical data regarding your device.

Contact Information: We collect contact details, including personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and address.

Payment Details: Your payment information can be stored within your Shirt Moxy account or collected in relation to an order, return, promotion, contest, or fundraising event.

Call Records, Email Records, Text Records, and Chat Logs: If you communicate with our customer service agents via calls, emails, texts, or chats, we may retain records of those conversations.

Shopping History: We maintain records of your purchase history, including the items you buy and the items saved in your cart or shopping bag.

Website/App Interactions: We gather information about your interactions with our Platforms, such as browsing and search history, visited pages, clicked links and buttons, mouse movements, scroll behavior, and non-sensitive keystroke activity. Depending on the information you provide, this data might also include personal information.

Submitted Content: Any content you submit to our Platforms, such as photos, videos, or reviews, is collected by us. For more details about user-generated content, please refer to our Endorsements and Social Media Engagement policy.

Demographic Information: We may gather demographic information about you, including your birthdate, height and weight, gender, ZIP code, and similar details.

Social Media Usage: We receive aggregated information about individuals who visit and engage with our Social Media Pages. "Aggregated" refers to information that does not include personal information linked to identified individuals.
